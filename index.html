Institution snapshot

Public institutions that show up across Göteborg's nature work

Older reserve pages may still mention Park- och naturförvaltningen. Göteborgs Stad states that function was split into Stadsmiljöförvaltningen and Exploateringsförvaltningen in the 2023 reorganisation.

Länsstyrelsen Västra Götaland (Västra Götaland County Administrative Board)

Usually manages protected nature such as nature reserves and national parks, and remains a key authority for protected coastal and inland nature.

Stadsbyggnadsnämnden (City Planning Committee)

Leads municipal reserve formation, boundaries, strategic prioritisation, and skötselplaner for new city reserves.

Stadsmiljönämnden & Stadsmiljöförvaltningen (Urban Environment Committee & Administration)

Manage the city's natural areas, public bathing sites, and much of the day-to-day upkeep of forests, trails, bridges, wetlands, and outdoor facilities.

Miljöförvaltningen (Environmental Administration)

Provides environmental supervision and co-defines the guidance used for discharges to water recipients and the stormwater network.

Kretslopp och vatten (Recycling and Water)

Works with Miljöförvaltningen on water quality target values and supports planning around dagvatten, ditches, and watercourses.

Exploateringsförvaltningen (Land Development Administration)

Owns and develops city agricultural land and urban farming programmes that intersect with reserve-edge landscapes and biodiversity work.

Responsibility matrix

Area-by-area accountability

This view stays at area-type level. It does not try to assign parcel-level accountability.

Protected nature

Nature reserves & protected landscapes

Responsible authority
Länsstyrelsen Västra Götaland for most protected nature, with Göteborgs Stad acting as the public authority and manager for municipal reserves.
Responsible actors
Stadsbyggnadsnämnden handles reserve formation, boundaries, and skötselplaner; Stadsmiljönämnden and Stadsmiljöförvaltningen manage and develop reserves after formation; some reserve pages name Göteborgs Stad directly as naturvårdsförvaltare.
Daily maintenance
Skötselplan-led work includes gallring, wetland restoration, upkeep of trails and bridges, and follow-up so reserve goals are actually delivered on the ground.
Research units
Gothenburg Global Biodiversity Centre (GGBC) and the Department of Biological and Environmental Sciences support biodiversity, conservation, and ecosystem-service knowledge.

Sources: Länsstyrelsen protected nature, city reserve roles, Stora Amundö reserve page

Marine edge

Coast, archipelago & marine areas

Responsible authority
Göteborgs Stad for municipal bathing sites and many city-managed coastal sites, Länsstyrelsen for protected coastal nature, and Miljöförvaltningen with Kretslopp och vatten for water-quality guidance.
Responsible actors
Stadsmiljönämnden owns the municipal bathing-site mandate, Stadsmiljöförvaltningen runs the operational service, and Miljöförvaltningen plus Kretslopp och vatten set the guidance used for polluted water releases to recipients and networks.
Daily maintenance
During bathing season the city keeps toilets, bridges, lifebuoys, and containers in place, staffs larger sites with attendants, cleans most bathing sites daily, and can increase maintenance from 15 May when demand rises.
Research units
The Department of Marine Sciences, the Centre for Sea and Society, and the Swedish Institute for the Marine Environment provide marine science, cross-sector collaboration, and ecosystem-based governance support.

Sources: badplats responsibility and service, daywater roles, Department of Marine Sciences, Centre for Sea and Society, Swedish Institute for the Marine Environment

Land systems

Forests, urban natural areas & recreation corridors

Responsible authority
Stadsmiljönämnden through Stadsmiljöförvaltningen is the main public manager for the city's forests, natural areas, and outdoor public-space systems.
Responsible actors
Stadsmiljöförvaltningen has the city's broad public-space and natural-area mandate and runs work that must balance recreation, biodiversity, and cultural landscape values.
Daily maintenance
Work is area-specific, guided by mapped tasks and site instructions, with each area's natural conditions and public use shaping how maintenance is carried out with minimal disturbance.
Research units
BioEnv's plant ecology and environmental science work covers forests, land use, and urban vegetation, while GGBC links biodiversity science to restoration and practice.

Sources: natural-area maintenance, stadsmiljö remit, PEPES, GGBC

Freshwater link

Lakes, streams, wetlands & stormwater-linked habitats

Responsible authority
Göteborgs Stad is responsible for meeting water environmental quality norms, with Miljöförvaltningen and Kretslopp och vatten defining core guidance and Stadsmiljöförvaltningen managing the operational public systems.
Responsible actors
Miljöförvaltningen and Kretslopp och vatten set target values for polluted-water releases; Stadsmiljöförvaltningen maintains stormwater facilities and the connected public realm that influences downstream habitats.
Daily maintenance
Dagvattenanläggningar need regular drift and maintenance with explicit drift instructions so the treatment and delay functions keep working for streams, lakes, wetlands, and coastal receivers.
Research units
The Department of Biological and Environmental Sciences is the clearest research match here because its work spans land, freshwater, and marine environments.

Sources: water norms and guidance, stormwater maintenance, BioEnv research areas
